<p><p>The aerial order picker market is categorized into electric and semi-electric types. Electric aerial order pickers are fully powered by electric motors, offering higher efficiency and ease of use for vertical lifting and maneuvering, ideal for large warehouses. Semi-electric aerial order pickers combine manual and electric features, requiring operator input for movement while using electric power for lifting. This variant balances cost and functionality, making it suitable for smaller operations that need versatility without complete automation.</p></p>

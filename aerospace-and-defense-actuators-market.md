<p><p>The Aerospace and Defense Actuators Market comprises two main types: rotary actuators and linear actuators. Rotary actuators convert energy into rotational motion, primarily used in applications like control surfaces and landing gear in aircraft. Linear actuators, on the other hand, produce straight-line motion, essential for mechanisms such as flaps, cargo doors, and weapon systems. Both actuator types are crucial for enhancing the efficiency, responsiveness, and precision of aerospace and defense technologies, driving innovations in aircraft and military equipment.</p></p>

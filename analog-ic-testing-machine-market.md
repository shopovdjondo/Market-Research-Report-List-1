<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Analog IC Testing Machine market encompasses two primary types: Wafer Testers and Packaged Device Testers. Wafer Testers evaluate integrated circuits while they are still on the silicon wafer, allowing for high throughput and early defect detection. In contrast, Packaged Device Testers assess individual chips after they are housed in protective packaging, ensuring their performance and reliability under operational conditions. Both testing types are essential for maintaining quality and efficiency in the production of analog integrated circuits.</p></p>

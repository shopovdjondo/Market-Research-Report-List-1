<p><p>Analog multiplexers and demultiplexers are essential electronic components used for routing analog signals from multiple sources to a single output or vice versa. An analog multiplexer allows the selection of one of several input signals and directs it to a single output, while a demultiplexer takes a single input signal and channels it to one of several outputs. These devices are crucial in applications such as telecommunications, data acquisition systems, and audio/video processing, where efficient signal management is vital.</p><p>The Analog Multiplexers & Demultiplexers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period.

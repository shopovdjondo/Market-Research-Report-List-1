<p><strong>The Automotive Air Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air flow meter market is primarily segmented into two types: analog and digital. Analog air flow meters operate using mechanical components to measure airflow, providing real-time data through voltage changes. They are simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, digital air flow meters employ advanced sensors and microprocessors to deliver precise, high-resolution measurements. They offer enhanced accuracy, integration with vehicle systems, and diagnostics capabilities, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles, where performance and efficiency are critical.</p></p>

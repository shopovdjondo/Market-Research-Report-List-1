<p><strong>The Automotive Injector Nozzle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The automotive injector nozzle market can be categorized into several types based on fuel delivery systems. Sequential Fuel Injection (SFI) involves injecting fuel into each cylinder at timed intervals, optimizing combustion. Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber for improved efficiency and power. A combination of SFI and GDI (SFI+GDI) leverages both technologies for enhanced performance. Dual Direct Injection (DDI) utilizes both direct and port fuel injection for maximized efficiency and reduced emissions, catering to diverse engine designs.</p></p>

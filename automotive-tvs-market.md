<p><strong>The Automotive TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market is divided into two main types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS devices. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect circuits from voltage transients in a single polarity direction, commonly used in applications where negative voltage spikes are not a concern. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can handle transients in both positive and negative directions, making them suitable for more complex circuits with potential bidirectional voltage conditions. Both types are essential for safeguarding automotive electronics.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Continuous Autotransfusion Systems</li><li>Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autotransfusion Systems (ATS) are medical devices used to collect, process, and reinfuse a patient’s own blood during surgery or trauma, minimizing the need for donor blood. The market is categorized into Continuous and Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems. Continuous ATS operate in a seamless manner, providing real-time processing and reinfusion, while Discontinuous ATS collect blood at intervals, processing it later. Both systems aim to enhance patient safety, reduce transfusion-related complications, and promote faster recovery in surgical settings.</p></p>

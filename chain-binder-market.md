<p><p>Chain binders are essential tools used in securing cargo during transportation. The market is primarily segmented into two types: ratchet binders and lever binders. Ratchet binders utilize a ratchet mechanism for tightening the chain, allowing for precise tension adjustments and secure binding, making them ideal for heavy loads. Lever binders, on the other hand, utilize a lever arm to operate the binder, offering ease of use and quick application, often preferred for lighter duties and frequent adjustments in cargo handling.</p></p>

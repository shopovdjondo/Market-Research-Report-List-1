<p><p>The commercial aircraft leasing market primarily includes two types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves leasing an aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance, allowing airlines to operate with more operational flexibility and control. Both leasing types offer distinct advantages, catering to various operational needs and financial strategies in the airline industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Commercial Utility Fans and Ventilation Blowers market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugal fans and blowers, and axial fans and blowers. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotatable impeller to move air radially, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, axial fans push air parallel to the fan shaft, suitable for lower pressure applications and high airflow rates. Both types serve essential roles in commercial settings, ensuring effective ventilation, temperature control, and air circulation.</p></p>

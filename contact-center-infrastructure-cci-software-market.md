<p><p>Contact Center Infrastructure (CCI) software market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. In contrast, on-premises CCI involves installing software directly on local servers, providing greater control, customization, and security, but typically requiring higher initial costs and maintenance. Both types serve different organizational needs based on size, budget, and operational preferences.</p></p>

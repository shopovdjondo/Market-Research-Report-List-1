<p><p>Data center air conditioning systems primarily include air-cooled, water-cooled, and other cooling solutions. Water-cooled systems, on the other hand, employ water as a heat exchange medium, offering higher cooling efficiency, especially in large-scale operations. Other market types may encompass specialized cooling methods like evaporative cooling or containment solutions, providing tailored options to meet the diverse thermal management needs of data centers.</p></p>

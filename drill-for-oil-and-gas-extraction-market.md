<p><p>The oil and gas extraction market employs various types of drills, notably Fixed Cutting Edge Drills and Cone Drills. Fixed Cutting Edge Drills feature rigid, blade-like edges that efficiently penetrate rock formations, providing a stable and controlled drilling process. In contrast, Cone Drills utilize rotating conical teeth to crush and displace material, making them ideal for softer rock conditions. Each type serves specific geological environments and operational needs, contributing to the overall efficiency and effectiveness of resource extraction.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Diamond Drilling Head</li><li>Cone Drilling Head</li><li>Drag Drilling Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling head market consists of three primary types: Diamond Drilling Heads, Cone Drilling Heads, and Drag Drilling Heads. Diamond Drilling Heads use robust diamond-tipped bits for precision and durability, ideal for hard rock formations. Cone Drilling Heads utilize rotating cones to break through various materials, suitable for diverse geological conditions. Drag Drilling Heads feature flat blades that slide across the surface, effectively removing material. Each type serves specific applications across mining, construction, and geological exploration, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>

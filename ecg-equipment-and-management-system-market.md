<p><p>The ECG Equipment and Management System market encompasses various devices used to monitor and assess heart activity. Holter Monitors provide continuous ECG data over 24-48 hours for ambulatory patients. Resting ECG Systems capture heart electrical activity at rest, often in clinical settings. Stress ECG Monitors evaluate heart performance during exercise or stress tests. Event Monitoring Systems allow patients to record ECG data during symptomatic episodes, enabling targeted analysis. Together, these systems enhance cardiac diagnostics and patient management.</p></p>

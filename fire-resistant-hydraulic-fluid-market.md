<p><strong>The Fire-Resistant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant hydraulic fluid market is divided into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing emulsions providing excellent fire resistance. HFB fluids are also emulsions but with less water content. HFC fluids are water-containing solutions with additives, offering good fire resistance while operating under high temperatures. HFD fluids are non-water-containing and typically composed of synthetic oils, providing superior fire protection and thermal stability. Each type serves specific applications in industries where fire hazards are significant.</p></p>

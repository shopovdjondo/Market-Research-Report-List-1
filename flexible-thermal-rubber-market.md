<p><p>The Flexible Thermal Rubber market is categorized into NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based, and Chloroprene based rubbers, among others. NBR is known for its oil resistance and durability, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. EPDM offers excellent weather resistance and flexibility, suitable for roofing and seals. Chloroprene, or Neoprene, provides good chemical stability and is used in diverse applications, including electrical insulation. Other types include specialized compounds tailored for specific needs.</p></p>

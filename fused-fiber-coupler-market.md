<p><p>The Fused Fiber Coupler market primarily consists of Single Mode Couplers and Multimode Couplers. Single Mode Couplers are designed for light transmission through a single optical fiber, suitable for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, Multimode Couplers cater to systems using multiple modes of light within a larger core fiber, enabling higher data rates over shorter distances. Both types are essential for various applications in telecommunications, data centers, and fiber-optic networks, facilitating efficient signal distribution.</p></p>

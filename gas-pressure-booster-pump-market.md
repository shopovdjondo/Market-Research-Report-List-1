<p><p>Gas pressure booster pumps are essential for enhancing gas pressure in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage booster pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low-pressure applications, efficiently increasing gas pressure in a single pass. In contrast, multi-stage pumps are used for high-pressure requirements, consisting of several impellers that enable incremental pressure increases, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes. Both types serve critical roles in optimizing gas delivery systems.</p></p>

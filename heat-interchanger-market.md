<p><p>The heat exchanger market comprises various types, including shell and tube, plate, fin type, and air-cooled heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes, facilitating efficient heat transfer across fluids. Plate heat exchangers use thin plates for compact designs and high effectiveness. Fin type heat exchangers enhance surface area for improved heat transfer, ideal for gas-to-liquid applications. Air-cooled heat exchangers utilize air for cooling, making them efficient for various industrial processes, particularly in outdoor setups.</p></p>

<p><p>The IGBT induction furnace market is primarily categorized into two types: the Industrial Frequency (IF) market and the High Frequency (HF) market. IF induction furnaces operate at lower frequencies, typically around 50-60 Hz, and are ideal for large-scale melting applications, offering efficient metal processing. In contrast, HF induction furnaces operate at higher frequencies, allowing for quicker heating and precise melting, making them suitable for smaller components and specialized applications. Both markets cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing productivity and energy efficiency.</p></p>

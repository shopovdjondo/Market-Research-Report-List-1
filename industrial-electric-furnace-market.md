<p><p>The industrial electric furnace market primarily includes two types: the industrial electric arc furnace and the industrial induction furnace. Electric arc furnaces utilize high-voltage electric arcs to melt metal, making them efficient for recycling scrap steel and producing high-quality steel. In contrast, induction furnaces use electromagnetic induction to heat and melt metal, offering precise temperature control and energy efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industries, including steel and non-ferrous metal production, driving advancements in manufacturing processes.</p></p>

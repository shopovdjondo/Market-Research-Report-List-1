<p><p>The inorganic adhesives market can be categorized into several types based on their chemical composition. Silicate type adhesives utilize sodium or potassium silicates for strong bonding and heat resistance. Sulfate type adhesives primarily incorporate calcium sulfate for their binding properties, often used in construction. Phosphate type adhesives utilize metal phosphates, offering excellent thermal stability. Borate type adhesives, containing boron compounds, provide antifungal and fire-resistant traits. Additionally, other types encompass various inorganic compounds tailored for specific applications in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>

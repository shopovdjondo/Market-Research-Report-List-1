<p><p>The ISO 5199 pumps market primarily encompasses two types: mag-drive pumps and sealed pumps. Mag-drive pumps operate without traditional seals, relying on magnetic coupling to prevent leakage, making them ideal for handling hazardous or corrosive fluids. In contrast, sealed pumps utilize mechanical seals to contain the fluid, providing a robust solution for various applications. Both pump types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing reliability, efficiency, and optimal performance in fluid transfer scenarios, with differing advantages based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>

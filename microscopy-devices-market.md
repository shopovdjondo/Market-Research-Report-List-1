<p><p>The microscopy devices market includes several types, each serving unique purposes. Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPMs) offer high-resolution imaging at the nanoscale by scanning a probe over a sample's surface. Electron Microscopes utilize electron beams for detailed imaging at much higher resolutions than optical counterparts, making them ideal for cellular structures. Optical Microscopes use visible light and lenses to magnify samples, suitable for biological studies. Other markets encompass advanced techniques like fluorescence and confocal microscopy, catering to specialized research needs.</p></p>

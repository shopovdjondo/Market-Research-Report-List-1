<p><ul><li>Cloud RAN</li><li>Centralized RAN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile fronthaul market primarily comprises two types: Cloud RAN and Centralized RAN. Cloud RAN decouples radio functions from hardware, allowing virtualization and centralized processing in the cloud for improved flexibility and resource management. Conversely, Centralized RAN consolidates baseband processing at a central location while managing multiple remote radio heads, facilitating enhanced performance and reduced latency. Both types aim to optimize network efficiency, support massive connectivity, and enable smoother transitions to 5G and beyond.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Solid-Liquid Interfaces</li><li>Solid-Solid Interfaces</li><li>Solid-Vapor Interfaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nanoengineered surfaces are categorized into three main market types based on their interfaces. Solid-liquid interfaces involve surfaces designed to enhance interactions with liquids, improving properties like wettability and adhesion, useful in applications like coatings and biomedical devices. Solid-solid interfaces focus on optimizing contact points between materials, enhancing strength and durability in composites and microelectronic devices. Solid-vapor interfaces pertain to surfaces interacting with gases, targeting applications in sensors and catalysts to manipulate adhesion and chemical reactions effectively.</p></p>

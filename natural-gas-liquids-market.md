<p><p>Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs) refer to a group of hydrocarbons extracted from natural gas, which include ethane, propane, isobutane, normal butane, and pentane. Ethane is primarily used for ethylene production in plastics. Propane serves as a heating fuel and feeds for petrochemicals. Isobutane is crucial for gasoline blending and refrigeration. Normal butane is often utilized in lighters and gasoline, while pentane is mainly used in the production of polystyrene and as a solvent. Each NGL has distinct market applications and demand drivers.</p></p>

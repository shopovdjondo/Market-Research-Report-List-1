<p><strong>The Plant-Based Egg Replacers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Egg White Replacement</li><li>Egg Yolk Replacement</li><li>Complete Egg Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plant-based egg replacers market consists of three primary categories: egg white replacement, egg yolk replacement, and complete egg replacement. Egg white replacements focus on mimicking the properties of egg whites, often using ingredients like aquafaba or mung bean protein for binding and aeration in recipes. Egg yolk replacements aim to replicate the richness and emulsifying properties of yolks, commonly using plant oils or starches. Complete egg replacements combine both aspects, providing a versatile substitute for whole eggs in various culinary applications.</p></p>

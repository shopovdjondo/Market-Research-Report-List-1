<p><strong>The Residential Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Ventilator</li><li>Negative Pressure Ventilator</li><li>Bidirectional Flow Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential ventilator market includes three primary types: Positive Pressure Ventilators, Negative Pressure Ventilators, and Bidirectional Flow Ventilators. Positive Pressure Ventilators push air into living spaces, ensuring fresh air circulation and improved indoor air quality. Negative Pressure Ventilators, on the other hand, create a vacuum effect to draw stale air out, enhancing ventilation efficiency. Bidirectional Flow Ventilators combine both functions, providing a balanced airflow by simultaneously expelling stale air and introducing fresh air, optimizing comfort and health in residential environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The set-top box remote control market comprises several types: Traditional Infrared (IR) remote controls, Wireless Radio Frequency (RF) remote controls, and others. Traditional IR remotes rely on line-of-sight communication, using infrared light to send signals to the set-top box. In contrast, RF remotes use radio waves, allowing operation from greater distances and without a direct line of sight. Other types may include Bluetooth or app-based controls, enhancing user experience by integrating smart technology and providing additional functionality.</p></p>

<p><p>The smart camera market primarily features two sensor types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for consumer electronics and portable devices. In contrast, CCD sensors offer superior image quality and low noise, often utilized in professional photography and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse market segments, with their unique strengths influencing consumer preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>

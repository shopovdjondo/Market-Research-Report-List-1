<p><p>Solar panel mounting structures are essential for securing solar panels, and they come in various types to suit different installations. Roof-mounted structures are directly affixed to building rooftops, optimizing space in urban areas. Ground-mounted structures are installed on the ground, often used in large-scale solar farms. Top-of-pole mounted structures elevate panels for optimal sun exposure, while side-of-pole mounted structures attach panels to vertical poles, balancing direct sunlight and space efficiency. Other market types may include dual-axis trackers and specialized mounts for unique applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The solar tracking system market primarily features two types: single-axis and dual-axis solar tracking systems. Single-axis systems rotate on one axis, either horizontally or vertically, allowing panels to follow the sun's path throughout the day, enhancing energy capture by about 25-35%. Dual-axis systems, on the other hand, adjust both horizontally and vertically, optimizing solar exposure year-round and increasing efficiency by 35-45%.

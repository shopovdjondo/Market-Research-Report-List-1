<p><p>Swing access gates are categorized into two market types: automatic and manual. Automatic swing gates utilize motors and sensors for hands-free operation, ideal for high-traffic areas as they enhance convenience and security. They can be integrated with various access control systems. In contrast, manual swing gates require physical effort to open and close, making them suitable for lower-traffic environments. While they are typically more cost-effective, they offer less convenience and require diligent maintenance to ensure proper functionality.</p></p>

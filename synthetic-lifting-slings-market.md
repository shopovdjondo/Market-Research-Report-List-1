<p><p>The synthetic lifting slings market includes two primary types: round slings and web slings. Web slings consist of flat synthetic material, providing strength and versatility in various configurations, suitable for different lifting applications. Both types are lightweight, durable, and resistant to environmental factors, making them popular choices across industries like construction, manufacturing, and logistics.</p></p>

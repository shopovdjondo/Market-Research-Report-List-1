<p><p>Telepresence robotics encompasses two main market types: mobile telepresence robots and stationary telepresence robots. Mobile telepresence robots are designed for dynamic interactions, allowing users to navigate different environments remotely, making them ideal for meetings, educational purposes, and healthcare. In contrast, stationary telepresence robots are fixed in one location, facilitating communication and interaction from a set point, commonly used in offices and reception areas. Both types enhance remote engagement, each serving unique operational needs and user experiences.</p></p>

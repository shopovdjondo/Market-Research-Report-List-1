<p><p>The termite bait system products market comprises two primary types: bait devices and liquid eradication methods. Bait devices involve strategically placed stations that attract and poison termites, effectively targeting colonies over time. This method is environmentally friendly and minimizes chemical exposure. In contrast, liquid eradication methods use chemical treatments applied to soil or structures to eliminate termites instantly. Each method caters to different consumer preferences, with bait devices often favored for long-term prevention, while liquid methods provide rapid response solutions.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way TPMS</li><li>Two-way TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) are designed to monitor and report tire pressure levels, ensuring optimal safety and performance. There are two primary market types: one-way TPMS and two-way TPMS. One-way TPMS transmits tire pressure data to the vehicle's display without receiving signals back, typically more cost-effective. In contrast, two-way TPMS communicates bidirectionally, allowing for additional features like real-time pressure adjustments and alerts, enhancing overall user experience and safety. Both systems are crucial for efficient vehicle maintenance.</p></p>
